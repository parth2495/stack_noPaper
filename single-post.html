How internet can help SMEs(Small & Medium Enterprises)?


What counts as SMEs in India?

According to Indian standards, enterprises where the investment in plant and machinery or equipments is between Rs. 25 lakhs ( ͌ US $ 0.04 million) to Rs. 10 crores ( ͌ US$1.6 million) in case of a manufacturing industry and between Rs. 10 lakh ( ͌ US $ 0.02 million) to Rs. 5 Crore ( ͌ US $ 0.8 million) in case of a service sector enterprise are all understood as small and medium scale enterprises.


How SMEs can achieve growth?

SMEs can use different marketing techniques and enhance their customer services/support to achieve growth in their Industry.


How Internet provides resources for growth?

For SMEs to grow their business, they can use the following Strategies:

1. Spreading Knowledge/Awareness

There are number of platforms like Facebook, Pinterest, LinkedIn, Reddit, Quora, Youtube, Instagram, etc where SMEs can share some benefits of their Products/Services or can share new ideas/solutions to customer’s problems. This kind of strategy not only helps SMEs getting new customers who may find it helpful but can make their content viral which will lead to massive popularity.


2. Networking

Currently, Most of clients SMEs gets are through their contacts (who are familiar with each other through some reference). SMEs can use same methods of Networking using Internet to get new Clients. As they can use Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn ,etc to create their company profile and then ask their current contacts to share and let their contacts know about your current work which after some level leads to big numbers.


3. Customer Support

As we have seen there are several social media platforms like whatsapp, facebook messenger which can be used for free by SMEs. They can use it to Provide continuous support to their clients or can solve any new problem given by their clients not only to sustain their Business with them but also to get some new clients as recommendations for their amazing Support.


4. Feedbacks

SMEs can ask for feedbacks in their current procedures and can improve accordingly to solve client’s problem or fulfil client’s requirement more accurately.


Conclusion

So, This is how SMEs can use Internet to grow their Business. There are lots of other ways to grow. But we have focused only those which won’t cost more for SMEs.
