Features

Our Product is best suited for small and medium business owners. We have created perfect product with below mentioned features.

Free & Secure Storage

Provides unlimited storage and high security with no extra charges. User controls all business data.

Analytics

Generate analytics to know insights of your Business, Accounts, Items and Tax in easy to understand manner.

Mobility

Provides dashboard where multiple Companies, Orders, Payment records, can be managed from any device and anywhere.

Repository

Manages your Repository automatically(for Selling and Purchase) and shows you alert when you will reach limit set by you.

Invoice

Generate PDFs for your sells or purchase order bills with GST and also monitor payments by date for each order.
